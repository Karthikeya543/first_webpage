Ferrari LaFerrari


The 2013 Ferrari LaFerrari introduced a hybrid system combining a V12 engine and electric motor for 963 hp, advanced aerodynamics, F1-derived technologies, a carbon-fiber monocoque, and a digital instrument panel. It was Ferrari's most powerful and innovative road car at the time.


The Ferrari LaFerrari was created by a talented team led by Flavio Manzoni, who was the director of Ferrari’s Styling Centre. The project brought together the expertise of Ferrari's GT and F1 engineers, resulting in a car that integrated advanced aerodynamics, hybrid technology, and cutting-edge design۔

Ferrari produced a total of 499 units of the LaFerrari coupé from 2013 to 2016. Additionally, they made 210 units of the LaFerrari Aperta, the open-top version, between 2016 and 2018.
