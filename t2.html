McLaren P1


The McLaren P1 is a groundbreaking hypercar with a hybrid powertrain combining a 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8 engine and an electric motor, generating 903 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.8 seconds and reaches a top speed of 350 km/h.

Its advanced aerodynamics, active suspension, and lightweight carbon fiber construction enhance performance and handling.

Limited to only 375 units, the P1 boasts a futuristic design optimized for aerodynamics. The blend of cutting-edge technology, exceptional performance, and striking design makes it one of the most iconic hypercars ever created.
